About ARMP

The Austrian Research Metadata Platform (ARMP) aggregates research publications, projects, and researcher information from Austria's leading universities and research institutions.

Data Sources

  • OpenAIRE - European research infrastructure aggregating publications and datasets
  • Crossref - Scholarly publication metadata database
  • ORCID - Researcher identification and profiling
  • FWF Research Radar - Austrian Science Fund project data

Coverage

ARMP includes research outputs from 15+ major Austrian research institutions including the University of Vienna, TU Wien, University of Innsbruck, University of Graz, and many others.

Mission

To make Austrian research discoverable, comparable, and accessible to researchers, institutions, and policymakers worldwide.
